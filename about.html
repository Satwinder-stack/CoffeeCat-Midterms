Welcome to Coffee Cat Café!

Coffee Cat Café is a cozy haven where coffee lovers and cat enthusiasts unite. Nestled in the heart of the city, our café offers a unique experience: enjoy premium coffee and delicious treats while spending time with our adorable resident cats.

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by a group of passionate animal lovers, Coffee Cat Café was inspired by the desire to create a relaxing space where people can unwind, connect, and support animal welfare. Our mission is to provide a safe and loving environment for rescue cats while offering a memorable café experience for our guests. The cats were adopted when they were just kittens and some were rescued.

Our Mission

  • Serve high-quality, ethically sourced coffee and food.
  • Promote the adoption and well-being of rescue cats.
  • Foster a sense of community and relaxation for all visitors.
